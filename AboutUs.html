About Kappy Web you ask?
We are passionate Web Developers who aid businesses aswell as people that are on the search for new digital heights.

If you want a brand new static website that looks beautiful, is reliable and interactive, we are here for you!

Appear on Google searches you say?  We are here for you!

Get that domain name you wanted all along and the professional emails connected with it? We are here for you!

Whether you're a student looking to showcase your work, a business owner looking to attract clients, or a graphic artist looking to share your projects, we are here for you!

So, join us on our endeavour!



We will build a fully functional and responsive website for you, this will be done using HTML, CSS, JavaScript and by harnessing the power of Bootstrap libraries.

As for hosting, your website will be hosted on Amazon Web Services S3 Buckets, backed up by Amazon Cloudfront (a web service that speeds up the distribution of your static content).

The SSL Certificate is a major part of websites, specially nowadays, so we highly recommend you to have one on your website. We use Amazon Certificate Authority in order to emit our SSL Certificates for your domain.

As for the rest, Cookies, Google Analytics, and SEO, we will help you setting them up, depending on the package you choose, of course.
